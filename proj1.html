HepAssist Mobile Application

The researchers of Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila will propose a mobile application that is accessible to all users who have hepatitis A, B, and C due to its ease of use and user friendliness. It allows the user to monitor their health and take precautions when necessary. The app tracks one’s health and is simple and easy to understand. It will assist users in getting the support they need and tracking their daily dose of medicines.
